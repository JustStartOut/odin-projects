Chicken Masala

Description

Ingredients:
  • Boneless Chicken Thighs (or Breasts)
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Kashmiri Chili Powder (or Red Chili Powder)
  • Butter
  • Onions (Finely Diced)
  • Heavy Cream

Steps:
  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Sear The Chicken
  3. Make the Tikka Masala Sauce
  4. Combine the Sauce Ingredients
  5. Add Cream and Chicken
  6. Final Touches and Serving
